About Us

A little piece of home in District 1.

KnK Inn sits right on De Tham — the backpacker street — two minutes from Bui Vien's neon and ten minutes from Ben Thanh Market. We built the place we wanted to drink in: an Aussie pub that actually feels like one, with a serious coffee bar, and rooms upstairs that don't pretend to be anything other than comfortable and quiet.

Six floors. A rooftop garden that catches the breeze. Every match on every screen. Come for the cricket, stay for the wine list.

Our Rooftop Garden

Up above the chaos.

Climb three flights and the city changes. Frangipani, potted lemongrass, slow fans, low lights — a pocket of green that feels nothing like District 1 below.

It's where we host sundowners, long lunches, quiet coffees and the best seat in the house for a Saigon thunderstorm. Open to guests from sunrise until late.

  • Sunset cocktails from 5pm
  • Private hire for small groups
